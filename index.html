Taki Hasan Rafi

PhD Student in Computer Science at HYU.

I am a PhD student in Computer Science in the Department of Computer Science , Hanyang University. I am advised by Prof. Dong-Kyu Chae at Data Intelligence Laboratory as a Graduate Student Researcher.

More about me
Before joining to HYU, I previously worked with Prof. Young Woong Ko as a Researcher at Operating System Laboratory , Hallym University. Prior to this, I received Bachelor's Degree from the Department of Electrical Engineering at Ahsanullah University of Science and Technology in August 2021. I am broadly interested in developing meta-learning methods for computer vision tasks with application to healthcare domain. My undergrad thesis work was focused on the applications of spiking neural networks. Besides research, I worked at Summit Communication Limited as an Apprentice Engineer and I also worked as a STEM Educator at SciTech Innovations Limited , where I contributed developing a student-friendly STEM curriculum first time in Bangladesh. My PhD is partially supported by Brain Korea 21 Plus!
